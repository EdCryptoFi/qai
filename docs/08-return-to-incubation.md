# Return to Incubation

## Primary Mechanism: Revenue Share (Capped at 1.25x)

QAI charges 1% on bonding curve trades. Qubic receives a share.

### Terms

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| **Calculation base** | Gross trading fee revenue (1% of bonding curve volume) |
| **Qubic share (pre-cap)** | 50% of gross fees |
| **Cap** | **1.25x** the total funded amount ($22,500) |
| **Duration** | Until cap is reached (no time limit) |
| **Post-cap** | Qubic receives 10% of fees in perpetuity |
| **Reporting** | Monthly on-chain + public dashboard |
| **Start trigger** | M4 completion (launch) |

### Why Trading Fees Make This Realistic

The bonding curve model generates **volume, not just creation value**.

| Metric | Creation-only model | Trading fee model |
|---|---|---|
| Fee source | 1% on token creation | 1% on every buy/sell |
| Volume multiplier | 1x | **50-200x** |
| Qubic share needed | $45,000 in fees | $45,000 in fees |
| Volume needed | $4.5M creation value | **$4.5M trading volume** |
| Feasibility | Hard (little creation) | **Easy (volume compounds)** |

### Example (based on $18,000 ask)

**Cap:** $22,500 (1.25x)
**Gross fees needed:** $22,500 ÷ 50% = $45,000
**Trading volume needed:** $45,000 ÷ 1% = $4,500,000

**Realistic scenario on Qubic's scale:**

| Scenario | Volume/token | Tokens needed | Timeframe |
|---|---|---|---|
| Conservative | $45,000 avg vol | 100 tokens | ~5 months (5/week) |
| Moderate | $90,000 avg vol | 50 tokens | ~2.5 months |
| One hit | $4.5M single token | 1 token | 1 week (if viral) |

### Comparison: Pump.fun on Solana

- Pump.fun launched in early 2024
- Within 6 months: **$100M+ cumulative fees**
- Average token: ~$200K lifetime volume
- Top tokens: $10M+ volume each
- **If Qubic captures even 0.1% of that dynamic, 1.25x is easily achievable**

## Post-Cap Distribution

After the 1.25x cap ($22,500) is reached:

| Recipient | Share | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Qubic Incubation | **10%** in perpetuity | Ongoing return to ecosystem |
| QAI protocol | **90%** in perpetuity | Development, infra, content, growth |

The 90/10 split ensures Qubic benefits indefinitely while QAI remains sustainable — covering Bob indexer VPS, IPFS pinning, domain, and continued development.

## Guardrails

| Rule | Compliance |
|---|---|
| Return from same source as value | ✅ Trading fees = value of the bonding curve |
| No vague payback language | ✅ Cap, base, trigger, duration defined |
| No token-only upside | ✅ Revenue share, no token needed |

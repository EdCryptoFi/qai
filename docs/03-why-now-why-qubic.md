# Why Now — Why Qubic

## Why Now

1. **Qubic mainnet is live but stagnant** — ~9 contracts in 2 years. The bottleneck is infrastructure, not demand.
2. **No token standard exists** — First-mover defines the category. In 6 months someone else will.
3. **Incubation Program is open** — This is the moment to get funded for foundational work.
4. **Cross-chain liquidity is coming** — SolanaBridge is in testnet. When it lands, Qubic will need tokens to bridge. QAI ensures they exist.
5. **Oracles arriving July 2026** — Smart contracts will need tokens to interact with. QAI provides the primitive.

## Why Qubic

| Feature | Token Advantage |
|---|---|
| **Feeless** | Transfer any token at zero cost. Microtransactions viable. |
| **Native assets** | `qpi.issueAsset()` is built-in. QAI just wraps it in a standard interface. |
| **Baremetal speed** | Token transfers confirm in ticks (~seconds), not blocks (~minutes). |
| **No gas wars** | No congestion pricing. Token operations don't fail due to gas spikes. |

## What Qubic gains from QAI

- **Bonding curve tokens** — Pump.fun-style activity. Tokens trade instantly, fees accrue, liquidity migrates to QSwap.
- **DEX volume** — Bonding curve tokens auto-migrate liquidity to QSwap AMM. More tokens = more DEX activity.
- **Adoption** — Lowers barrier from "C++ engineer" to "anyone with a browser"
- **Standards** — QRC20 + QRC721 enable wallets, explorers, and marketplaces to integrate once
- **Developer mindshare** — Clear standard + viral token creation attracts builders

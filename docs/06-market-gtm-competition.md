# Market, GTM & Competition

## Market

Qubic's token market is nonexistent — which is the opportunity.

- **Token standards:** None
- **Token creation tools:** None (C++ + PR + IPO required)
- **Active tokens:** ~9 core contracts + whatever QubicBay issues
- **DEX:** QX (order-book, $70K TVL) + QSwap (AMM, Uniswap V2 style)
- **Marketplace:** QubicBay

## Competition

### Direct

| Competitor | Threat | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Building custom SC per project | Medium | Possible but high friction. Most won't bother. |
| QubicBay extending to token creation | Low | They're focused on marketplace. Different scope. |

### Indirect

| Competitor | Threat | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Other L1s (Solana, Sui, Base) | Low | Different chains. Qubic's feeless is a genuine advantage. |

### Competitive Advantage

1. **Only bonding curve platform** — No other token with instant tradability on Qubic
2. **Feeless + fast** — Trading on bonding curve costs nothing in fees (except protocol's 1%). Compare: ETH gas can exceed trade value.
3. **Auto-liquidity on QSwap** — Tokens that hit cap automatically get DEX liquidity. No manual LP setup.
4. **Standard + tooling** — Not just a bonding curve (Pump.fun) or just a standard (OpenZeppelin), but both
5. **Content-driven GTM** — Existing audience (7K+ X, YouTube, Discord)
